- nextgen Tech

A three-day national-level summit aiming to create a FOSS-iverse comprising of workshops, sessions, competitions and an open source hackathon.

17th - 19th March, 2023

About enFOSS

enFOSS is a national level FOSS Summit curated carefully to help anyone in the tech world to delve into the FOSS Universe. The three-day event is crafted with emphasis on creating a unique perspective on the advantage and superiority of an open development process wired by collaboration. enFOSS is crafted with an ulterior motive of FOSStering the next-gen tech advancements. We believe this can be achieved by creating a symposium of FOSS initiatives in Kerala that can help foster students and professionals in the idea of open source contribution.

Our Venue

Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam, pioneered engineering education in central Kerala in 1961. Managed by the Mar Athanasius College Association and aiided by the Government of Kerala, the college was the first in Asia under Christian Management. MACE can offer its huge ethereal campus that can house such a colossal meetup. With its six well-equipped seminar halls, Open air theatre, Main College Auditorium, and Indoor stadium, MACE has the infrastructure to support multiple conferences simultaneously. The serene surroundings and clean air make this a perfect location for educational excellence. Kothamangalam is well connected by road from all parts of Kerala State.
